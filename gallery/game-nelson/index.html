Game, game, game and again game

Jason Nelson
Game, game, game and again game
2007

Nelson’s bizarre Game, game, game and again game mashes together retro Super Mario-style platforming with scribbles, explosions, and textual madness. The game detonates one’s expectations of how a poem, drawing, or game should look (or run), and in doing so tries to look beyond the clean functionality of digital media. Together with its sequel I made this. You play this. We are enemies., Game, game, game, and again game comes perilously close to encoding madness.
